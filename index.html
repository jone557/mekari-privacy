Information We Collect

Information you provide

We may collect information that you enter into the app, including:

  • Account and profile information
  • Financial records such as accounts, transactions, spending categories, budgets, and reports
  • Notes or text you choose to enter
  • Support messages you send to us

Information collected automatically

When you use the app, we may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data, if available
  • Usage events needed to keep the app working properly

Permissions and sensitive data

Mekari may request access to certain device features depending on what you use in the app. For example:

  • SMS access, if you choose to import transaction-related messages
  • Camera access, if you choose to scan receipts or documents
  • Notification access, if you choose to receive reminders or alerts

We do not use these permissions for unrelated purposes.

2

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the app
  • Show your financial summaries, reports, and insights
  • Sync your data across devices, if enabled
  • Generate AI-assisted advisor suggestions
  • Send reminders, alerts, or notifications you enable
  • Diagnose issues and improve performance
  • Respond to support requests
3

AI Advisor

Mekari may include AI-assisted features such as financial health summaries, behavior insights, and guided questions.

Important notes:
  • AI features are intended for informational purposes only
  • They are not financial, legal, tax, or investment advice
  • Local rule-based logic may be used to generate some insights instantly
  • On-device AI processing may be used for enhanced summaries or recommendations
  • We do not sell your financial data
4

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data only in limited cases:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app, such as authentication or cloud sync services
  • When required by law
  • To protect the security, integrity, or rights of the app and its users
5

Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your data.

Your data may be stored:

  • On your device
  • In cloud services you enable, such as Firebase-backed sync

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we work to protect your information as much as reasonably possible.

6

Your Choices

You can:

  • Review and update your data in the app
  • Disable certain permissions through your device settings
  • Turn off notifications
  • Delete your account and associated data, where supported
7

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and app data by using the in-app deletion flow or by contacting us at the email below.

After deletion, we will remove data associated with your account, subject to legal, security, or operational retention requirements.

8

Children's Privacy

Mekari is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Storage / Firestore, if enabled
  • Google services required for Android distribution and app functionality

These services may process data according to their own privacy practices.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and may notify users in the app or on this page.
