Account Deletion

Users can delete their account directly inside the app.

Delete account in the app

Open the app and go to:

Settings > Delete Account

Confirm the deletion request. Your account and associated app data will be deleted.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

jhonemu342@gmail.com

Please include the email address or username associated with your account.

Data deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete your account information and associated app data, except any information we are required to keep for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.
