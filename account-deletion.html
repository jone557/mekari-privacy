Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users can delete their account and associated app data.

Delete your account in the app

You can delete your account directly from within the app at any time.

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your account deletion request.
Once confirmed, your account and associated app data will be deleted.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us.

jhonemu342@gmail.com

Please include the email address or username associated with your account so we can identify it.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete your account information and associated app data, including personal profile information and financial records stored by the app.

Some limited information may be retained only if required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance reasons.

Processing time

Account deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period after we receive and verify the request.
